FAQ

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions below.

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose is to provide users with tools to predict diseases, receive Ayurvedic medicine recommendations, and get doctor recommendations.

Is the information accurate and reliable?

We have gathered information from the internet, and while efforts are made to ensure accuracy, it's essential to exercise caution. For trustworthy advice, we recommend consulting with healthcare professionals before relying on the information presented here.

How accurate is the disease predicting model?

The accuracy varies depending on the disease but is generally high. However, it's not a substitute for a medical diagnosis.
